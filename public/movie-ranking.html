The Best Movies According to Emily

Unequivocally the best three movies according to a very scientific process (my own feelings and opinions)

  1. Spirited

    Expertly choreographed. Incredible score. Riotously funny. What more could you ask for from a made-for-TV musical?

  2. Cars 3

    It's a classic coming-of-age story retold for those learning to be teachers and celebrate the accomplishments of the next generation.

  3. The Princess Bride

    This is the film that inspired my own love story. I walked down the aisle to Storybook Love. It's got everything in one movie: Adventure, Wit, Romance, Humor... It truly is a gem.
